# Refund Policy
Refunds are available when a payment is duplicated or charged in error.
Refund approval is usually processed within 3 business days.
For duplicate payments, verify the order status and payment gateway capture record.

# Shipping Policy
Shipping delays beyond 72 hours require a courier check and fulfillment verification.
If the wrong product is delivered, request photo confirmation and issue a replacement or return label.

# Membership Policy
Premium members receive priority handling and faster resolution windows.
Cancellation requests must confirm the next billing cycle and any prorated charges.

# Cancellation Policy
Customers can cancel at any time before the renewal date.
Cancellation after renewal is subject to the current billing cycle policy.
